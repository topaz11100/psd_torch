The main PSD pipeline is executed as explicit stages:

src/model_training.py -> src/psd_analysis.py -> src/plotting.py
bash/model_training.sh -> bash/psd_analysis.sh -> bash/plotting.sh

Main-stage binary output is limited to selected `.pt` checkpoints written by src/model_training.py. Numeric artifacts are common-schema CSV files. PNG figures are produced only by src/plotting.py from CSV input.
